Explore Taniti Island with Convenient Transportation Options

Getting around Taniti Island is easy and convenient with a variety of transportation options available for visitors. Whether you prefer exploring by car, bike, or boat, Taniti offers flexible and accessible choices for all kinds of travelers.

Car Rentals

Explore the island at your own pace by renting a car. With numerous rental agencies located near the airport and in the main city, visitors can choose from a range of vehicles including sedans, SUVs, and electric cars. Most rental services offer daily or weekly rental options.

Bicycle Rentals

For a more eco-friendly and active way to see the island, rent a bicycle. Taniti Island has scenic cycling paths along the coast and through lush tropical landscapes. Bicycle rentals are available throughout the island, with daily and hourly rental rates.

Public Bus System

The island’s public bus system offers an affordable and convenient way to travel between major attractions, towns, and beaches. Buses run on a regular schedule, ensuring visitors have easy access to all parts of the island. The main bus terminal is located in the city center.

Taxis and Rideshares

For those seeking a more personalized mode of transport, taxis and rideshare services are readily available on the island. Taxis are conveniently located at key points like the airport, popular tourist spots, and resorts, offering door-to-door service.

Boat Rentals and Tours

For a unique island experience, consider renting a boat or joining a boat tour. Explore Taniti’s coastline, hidden coves, and nearby islets. Many boat rental services offer guided tours, fishing trips, and sunset cruises for a memorable adventure.

Airport Transfers

Airport transfers to and from the major resorts and accommodations can be arranged in advance. These transfers provide a hassle-free way to start your vacation. Shuttle services, private transfers, and group transports are available.
